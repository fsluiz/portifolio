About me

I'm a scientist with a passion for data analysis, programming, statistics, and quantum mechanics. Over the course of my career, I have worked on a wide range of projects that have allowed me to develop advanced knowledge in mathematics, statistics, physics, programming, and data analysis.

Some specific examples of my work include:

  • An extensive analysis of quantum states, known as coherent states, which led to the discovery of sets of states that increased the fidelity of the quantum teleportation protocol.
  • The insight to use these sets as an alphabet for the creation of a quantum encryption protocol.
  • Analysis of parameters in non-Hermitian Hamiltonians that led to the insight that changing this parameter takes the quantum thermodynamic system from cooler to engine.
  • Building a machine learning algorithm based on lasso algorithm to predict the revenue of each establishment in a fast-food chain network by analyzing sales data together with location and weather data during the brief existence of a startup.

    • Throughout these projects, I followed a consistent approach to data analysis:

    • Problem definition: identifying the questions we wanted to answer.
    • Data collection: in many cases, we created mathematical models to generate synthetic data when experimental data was not available or insufficient.
    • Data cleaning: checking for numerical errors, empty spaces, or duplicate data and ensuring that the data made sense.
    • Data analysis: checking for correlations between observables, answering the questions asked by the data, and gaining new insights.
    • Prediction: using mathematical models developed through data analysis to predict the behavior of the system.
    • Sharing results: publishing results in internationally indexed journals in several cases.
      • I believe that data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and quantum machine learning offer vast opportunities for technology development. Outside of work, I enjoy hiking and traveling to new places, which often provide inspiration for my work.
